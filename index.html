Welcome to Hotel Grand Surya

Hotel Grand Surya provides you all the comfort you need. It is located in the heart of city, just a few kilometers from Koderma Junction.

We introduce ourselves as one of the 3-Star business hotels in Koderma, situated in the busy area of Jhumritilaya. We provide the best hospitality service in town.

Elegantly furnished and located at one of the most convenient locations of Koderma, Hotel Grand Surya provides luxury hotel room hospitality services to business and leisure travelers. We have 28 well-furnished rooms.

Hotel Grand Surya provides quality accommodation to visitors coming to the city from different parts of the country and world. Strategic location, an assortment of amenities and services, and easy accessibility make our hotel highly sought after among guests.
